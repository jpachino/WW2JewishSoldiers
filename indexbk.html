WW2 Jewish Soldiers


Over 1.5 million Jewish Soldiers served in WW2 for the Allied countries. Over 500,000 from the United States, 500,000 from Russia and 500,000 from the rest of the Allied countries. Over 250,000 Jewish soldiers failed to come home. We honor the memory of the all the soldiers. This website is a portal to load information about our honored soldiers. They fought for the freedom of our world and we are ever in their debt.
